Each month we have four fun new books to read. Then we meet at the end of the month for a little get together and to share our favorite parts! Here are this months reads:

The Summer Girl

Cassie returns to Avalon Bay and wants a summer fling. She finds the perfect candidate: Tate, Avalon Bay's fun-loving golden boy. As Cassie and Tate walk the line between friends and lovers, they're about to discover that Avalon Bay is full of secrets - and their relationship might not survive when one thing in particular comes to light.

Silver Nitrate

This is a chilling story of haunted films and Nazi occultists in 1990s Mexico, as a mysterious auteur recruits a sound editor and her soap opera star friend to lift a curse from an unfinished movie.
